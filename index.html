Product Highlights

Investor Premier (1-4 Unit)

  • Min Credit Score: 660
  • Max LTV/CLTV: 80%
  • Loan Amount: $150K - $2M
  • Min DSCR: 0.75x (restrictions apply)

Investor Premier Elite

  • Min Credit Score: 680
  • Max LTV/CLTV: 80%
  • Loan Amount: $150K - $2M
  • Min DSCR: 1.00x (FICO dependent)

Prestige Plus

  • Min Credit Score: 680
  • Max LTV/CLTV: 90%
  • Loan Amount: $150K - $2.5M
  • Occupancy: Primary, Second, Investment

Prestige

  • Min Credit Score: 660
  • Max LTV/CLTV: 90%
  • Loan Amount: $150K - $3M
  • Occupancy: Primary, Second, Investment
